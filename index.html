Our Story

Plant Palette was created with a simple mission: to make plant-based dining vibrant, delicious, and sustainable. Founded by chef Tom, we source fresh, local ingredients to craft meals that nourish both people and the planet. More than a restaurant, we're a community where everyone—vegan or curious—can enjoy wholesome, flavorful food in a welcoming space.

Weekly lineup

Monday

Seasonal Vegetable Spring Rolls – Fresh, colorful vegetables wrapped in rice paper, paired with a creamy peanut dipping sauce. A light and fresh way to start the week.

Tuesday

Spicy Lentil Tacos – Soft corn tortillas filled with spiced lentils, topped with avocado salsa and pickled red onions, a perfect balance of flavors for taco lovers.

Wednesday

Plant Palette Burger – Our signature black bean and quinoa patty, topped with fresh avocado and house-made vegan aioli, served on a whole-grain bun.

Thursday

Roasted Cauliflower Steak – A thick slice of roasted cauliflower, served with chimichurri, sautéed kale, and brown rice. A hearty and nutritious dish.

Friday

Creamy Cashew Alfredo Pasta – Fettuccine tossed in a rich cashew cream sauce, mixed with seasonal veggies like zucchini and spinach. A creamy indulgence that's still plant-based.

Saturday

Quinoa Salad – A refreshing mix of quinoa, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and parsley, dressed in lemon vinaigrette. A light and fresh side or main to end the week.
